# Saurav R Tuladhar
I am a Staff Scientist at [ARCON Corporation](https://www.arcon.com) where I work on airspace terminal area automation systems. Design, analysis, tuning of algorithms used in the airspace surveillance system.
Over the last five years, I have worked on multiple projects which contribute to the transition of FAA's airspace surveillance system to the NexGen technology. Specifically I have worked on  
- Tuning and analysi of Interacting Multiple Model (IMM) filters used to track aircraft using measurements form radars and ADS-B.
- Implementing reference tracking algorithms as a part of RTCA's efforts to standardize requirements for the radar sensor on board UAVs. 

I 2016 completed my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering at the [University of Massachusetts Dartmouth](https://www.umassd.edu) working with [Dr. John R. Buck](https://www.umassd.edu/directory/jbuck/). As a part of my doctoral research I developed adaptive beamforming algorithms to suppress loud interferers in a training data starved scenarios. Specifically I designed a new technique to improve the minimum variance distortionless response (MVDR) beamformer algorithm.
